🌾 Smart Farmer AI

Smart Farmer AI (Kisan Mitra AI / Kisan.AI) is an AI-powered digital farming assistant designed to empower Indian farmers with intelligent, accessible, and multilingual agricultural guidance.

The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence to provide personalized recommendations on crop selection, soil health, weather forecasts, irrigation planning, pest and disease detection, fertilizer usage, and market price insights. It aims to simplify farming decisions, increase productivity, reduce costs, and promote sustainable agriculture.
Built with a farmer-first approach, Kisan.AI offers an easy-to-use interface supporting regional Indian languages, making modern AI technology accessible even in rural communities.

🌟 Key Features
🤖 AI-powered Farming Assistant
🌱 Smart Crop Recommendation
🌦️ Weather-Based Farming Alerts
💧 Irrigation & Water Management Guidance
🐛 AI Pest & Disease Detection
🌾 Soil Health & Fertilizer Recommendations
💹 Real-Time Market Price Information
🌍 Multilingual Support for Indian Farmers
📱 Mobile-Friendly & Easy-to-Use Interface

🎯 Mission
To bridge the gap between Artificial Intelligence and Indian agriculture by providing every farmer with a reliable digital companion that enables smarter decisions, higher yields, improved income, and sustainable farming practices.
